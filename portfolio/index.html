About Me

My journey into web development began at the age of 15, and it was a story of curiosity, trial, and pure fascination. Like most teenagers, I spent a lot of time online, browsing websites and playing games.
It wasn't long before I decided to dig deeper. Armed with nothing but a desire to understand and a few basic tutorials, I started learning HTML and CSS. The moment I saw my first web page come to life on my screen, I was hooked. I was amazed at how I could control every element on the page—from text and images to layout and design—just by writing a few lines of code. That feeling of creation, of making something tangible from nothing, was like magic to me.


My Works

I specialize in creating modern, responsive websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. My projects focus on clean design, seamless user experiences, and efficient coding practices. From personal portfolios to small business websites, I enjoy bringing ideas to life on the web with creativity and precision.
